tapist

tapist looks like a four-track tape looper, but with digital noises. It's a reaktor instrument. It's still in beta but you can try it.

You'll need a full version of Reaktor to add your own samples. Making it compatible with free Reaktor player with a pack of homemade samples is on my roadmap.

If you wanna hear some examples, listen to "tape" titled tracks in this /soundcloud playlist.

design

noisedata

noise:data is a prototype which contains a bunch of graphic modules sending OSC signals to control your favorite software. It's made to be seen during live performances.

The prototype was made in Processing. I'm currently switching to openframeworks for performance and time management but not entirely satisfied. Still looking for a good library to pursue development.
